About Jenny Sweet Treat

Founded by Jenny Okafor in 2015, we started as a small home bakery with a big dream: to bring joy through every bite. Today, we're Lagos's favorite stop for custom cakes, pastries, and small chops.

We use only the finest ingredients—Belgian chocolate, Madagascar vanilla, and farm-fresh dairy—to ensure every creation is as delicious as it is beautiful.
