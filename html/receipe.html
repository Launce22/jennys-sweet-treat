🎂 Classic Vanilla Sponge Cake

⏱️ 45 mins 👥 Serves 8 📊 Easy

Ingredients: Flour, sugar, butter, eggs, vanilla extract, baking powder, milk

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F (175°C). Grease and flour two 9-inch pans.
  2. Cream butter and sugar until light and fluffy (about 5 minutes).
  3. Add eggs one at a time, beating well after each addition.
  4. Mix in vanilla extract.
  5. Combine flour and baking powder, then alternate adding with milk.
  6. Bake for 25-30 minutes until a toothpick comes out clean.

🍪 Chewy Chocolate Chip Cookies

⏱️ 30 mins 👥 Makes 24 📊 Easy

Ingredients: Butter, brown sugar, white sugar, eggs, vanilla, flour, baking soda, salt, chocolate chips

  1. Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Beat melted butter with sugars until creamy.
  3. Add eggs and vanilla, mix well.
  4. Combine dry ingredients and fold into wet mixture.
  5. Stir in chocolate chips.
  6. Drop spoonfuls onto baking sheet and bake for 9-11 minutes.

🥐 Flaky Butter Croissants

⏱️ 3 hours 👥 Makes 12 📊 Advanced

Ingredients: Flour, sugar, yeast, salt, cold butter, milk, egg (for wash)

  1. Make dough with flour, sugar, yeast, salt, and milk. Rest for 1 hour.
  2. Roll cold butter into a flat sheet between parchment.
  3. Roll dough into rectangle, place butter sheet, fold and seal edges.
  4. Perform 3 turns (fold in thirds), resting 30 mins between each.
  5. Roll final dough, cut triangles, roll from base to tip.
  6. Curve into crescent shape, proof 1 hour, brush with egg wash.
  7. Bake at 400°F (200°C) for 18-20 minutes until golden.
